Get started
add coffee beans grinder

coffee bean are simply added to the grinder by lifting the grinder cover and pouring in the beans. The coffee grinder Ultracool 7000 must be at least 10% full to work properly. Add coffee beans to the Ultracool 7000 grinder every morning.

Here is information about the task.

This product can only be serviced by qualified professionals.

  1. Press Shut down to switch off the Ultracool 7000 Coffee and Tea Machine.
  2. Turn the grinder lock switch left until the Ultracool 7000 display shows Open.
  3. Lift the grinder cover.
  4. Pour in the coffee beans.
    When the grinder has enough beans, the display shows Full.

The water filter purifies the tap water before brewing the coffee or making the tea.

We added some context for experts.
Troubleshooting: Do something.
And prerequisites for novices only.
  1. Remove the filter from its packaging.
    Important: Watch out for slippery roads.
  2. Lift the water reservoir cover.
  3. Press the Open button.
  4. Pull the filter compartment towards you.
  5. Place the filter to the compartment carefully.
  6. Push the filter compartment away from you.
